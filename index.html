Projects

Headshots by JP Photography

JP Photography is owned and operated by Jordan Potter from Eagle, Idaho. Jordan has a strong passion for photography, and loves working with people. I reached out to him wanting to capture his personality and professionalism in a website. His site has a very modern, and open feel to it acting as a breath of fresh air for those who visit the site. The challenge with this site? Jordan wanted to find a way to host his website for free! The solution? An expertly crafted landing page hosted for free through GitHub Pages. The entire site is coded using vanilly HTML (with some Bootstrap) and CSS. I thoroughly enjoyed the challenge of creating this immersive, yet simple website.


Ashes To Apex

Ashes to Apex is a community resource for those entering the road of addiction recovery. Charles Leonhardt wrote about his personal struggles and journey in his book, Ashes to Apex. Following its publication he came to me looking to put his vision and passion for helping others into a website. Working with Wix Studios, I designed and built his website to give a welcoming, and inviting feel to all those who visit looking for help in recovery.


Z&R Custom Painting

Z&R Custom Painting is a premier commerical and residential painting company in the Treasure Valley. They do eveything from full exterior painting to expoxy flooring. They came to me simply wanting a place on the interent where their name could live. They aimed to target high-end clients, as chances to really showcase what they can do. My approach to this website was simple: give as many chances as possible to show off thier work. And with a website with nearly ten pages, that was easily done! I wanted visitors on their site to see they could trust Z&R with their home, and that trust surely shines through just on the landing page. This website was built and hosted by Strikingly.
